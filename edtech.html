Coding playground comes to WBAIS. The conception of this playground was conceived by Melinda Alford and I during our time as integration and learning coaches at Suzhou Singapore International School. Coding stations: Cubix, Sphero RVR+, Beebot, Mouse Robot, Osmo, Dash, Spike Essentials, Spike Prime and binary code bracelet.

Two in-house FIRST LEGO League competitions for a total of 25 FLL teams.

Design thinking is an important skill set in the 21st century. Creativity is unleashed in the process of design. The students sit at tables with whiteboard material to allow brainstorming during their design process.
